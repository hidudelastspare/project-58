Reading

As a leisure activity, children and adults read because it is pleasant and interesting. In the US, about half of all adults read one or more books for pleasure each year. About 5% read more than 50 books per year. Americans read more if they: have more education, read fluently and easily, are female, live in cities, and have higher socioeconomic status.[13] Children become better readers when they know more about the world in general, and when they perceive reading as fun rather than another chore to be performed.
